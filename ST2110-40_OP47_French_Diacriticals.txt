A Teletext subtitle decoder receiving the packets contained within
"ST2110-40_OP47_French_Diacriticals.pcap" should present the following on a
display:

  French diacritical marks:
  ç é âêîôû àèìòù ëïü

Note that all of these characters except ì, ò, and ü are contained within
the French National Option Sub-set table. Those remaining 3 characters
require an X/26 packet for composition.
