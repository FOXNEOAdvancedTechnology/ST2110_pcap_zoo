A Teletext subtitle decoder receiving the packets contained within
"french_chars.pcap" should present the following on a display:

  French diacritical marks:
  ç é âêîôû àèìòù ëïü

Note that all of these characters except ì, ò, and ü are contained within the
French National Option Sub-set table. Those remaining 3 characters require an
X/26 packet for composition.
